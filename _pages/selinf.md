#### Topics in Selective Inference and Predictive Inference
--- 

###### Syllabus 

- Multiple testing: global null, FWER, FDR 
- Testing with game-theoretic statistics: e-values 
- Competition-based testing 
- Compound decision-making

---  

###### Reading 
The following references are related to the topics covered in the class.

- Large-Scale Inference: Empirical Bayes Methods for Estimation, Testing, and Prediction by B. Efron, IMS Monographs.
- Hypothesis Testing with E-values by A. Ramdas and R. Wang, Foundations and Trends in Statistics. [pdf](https://sas.uwaterloo.ca/~wang/files/e-book-final.pdf)
