Welcome to my homepage!


I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.

From 2021-2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Statistics Department at the University of Chicago, advised by Professor Rina Foygel Barber. I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics from Stanford University, advised by Professor Emmanuel Candès. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Peking University. Here is a copy of my CV. You can also find me on Google scholar and GitHub.

I am currently a co-organizer of the International Seminar on Selective Inference (with Will Fithian, Jelle Goeman, Nikos Ignatiadis and Lihua Lei).
