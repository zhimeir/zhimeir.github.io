Welcome to my homepage!

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. From 2021-2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Statistics Department at the University of Chicago, advised by Professor Rina Foygel Barber. I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics from Stanford University, advised by Professor Emmanuel Candès. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Peking University.
